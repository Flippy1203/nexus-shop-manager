1. Overview

NEXUS Shop Manager is a private internal application used solely by the shop owner and authorized internal users to manage a single Etsy shop. This Privacy Policy explains what data is accessed, how it is used, and how it is protected.

2. Data We Access

To perform its core functions, the application accesses the following categories of data through official Etsy and manufacturing service integrations:

  • Shop data: Listings, inventory levels, pricing, and product details.
  • Order data: Customer names, shipping addresses, order items, and order status.
  • Fulfillment data: Tracking numbers, shipping carriers, and fulfillment status.
  • Account data: Shop settings and integration credentials required for API access.

3. How Data Is Used

Data is used exclusively for the internal operation of the shop:

  • Updating and organizing product listings.
  • Synchronizing inventory between systems.
  • Retrieving and fulfilling orders.
  • Generating shipping labels and tracking updates.

Data is not used for advertising, analytics profiling, or any purpose outside direct shop operations.

2. Data Sharing

Data is shared only with the minimum third-party services required to fulfill shop operations, including Etsy, Printify, and shipping providers. No data is sold, rented, or shared with unrelated parties.

4. Data Retention

Operational data is retained only as long as needed to manage the shop, fulfill orders, and comply with applicable tax and record-keeping requirements. When data is no longer needed, it is deleted or anonymized.

5. Security

Access to the application and its data is restricted to authorized internal users. API credentials are stored securely and are not exposed publicly. Communications with external services use encrypted channels where available.

6. Cookies and Tracking

This application does not use cookies for tracking, advertising, or analytics. No third-party tracking scripts are loaded. Session data, if any, is limited to maintaining authenticated access for authorized operators only.

7. Children's Privacy

This application is not directed to children under the age of 13. No personal information is knowingly collected from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in operations or legal requirements. The updated version will be posted at the same URL with a revised effective date.
