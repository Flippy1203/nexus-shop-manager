1. Overview

NEXUS Shop Manager is a private internal application used solely by the shop owner and authorized internal users to manage a single Etsy shop. This Privacy Policy explains what data is accessed, how it is used, and how it is protected.

2. Information We Access

To perform its core functions, the application accesses information through official Etsy and manufacturing service integrations. Access is scoped to the minimum permissions required for shop operations.

Information accessed may include:

  • Shop and listing data: Product listings, inventory levels, pricing, media URLs, and shop settings.
  • Order data: Customer names, shipping addresses, line items, order status, and timestamps.
  • Fulfillment data: Tracking numbers, shipping carriers, shipment status, and fulfillment timestamps.
  • Account and integration data: Shop identifiers and credentials required to maintain authorized API access.

3. How Information Is Used

Information is used exclusively for the internal operation of the authorized shop. Specific uses include:

  • Creating, updating, and organizing product listings.
  • Synchronizing inventory between the shop and manufacturing systems.
  • Retrieving orders and presenting them for review or fulfillment.
  • Generating shipping labels, updating tracking, and marking orders complete.

Information is not used for advertising, analytics profiling, marketing, or any purpose outside direct shop operations.

4. Data Sharing

Data is shared only with the minimum third-party services required to fulfill shop operations. These typically include Etsy, manufacturing or print partners, and shipping providers. No data is sold, rented, licensed, or shared with unrelated parties.

5. Data Retention

Operational data is retained only as long as needed to manage the shop, fulfill orders, and comply with applicable tax and record-keeping requirements. When data is no longer needed for these purposes, it is deleted or anonymized.

6. Security

Access to the application and its data is restricted to authorized internal users. API credentials are stored securely and are not exposed in public repositories, client-side code, or public networks. Communications with external services use encrypted channels where available.

The shop owner is responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of credentials and for all activity under authorized accounts.

7. Cookies and Tracking

This application does not use cookies for tracking, advertising, or analytics. No third-party tracking scripts are loaded. Session data, if any, is limited to maintaining authenticated access for authorized operators only.

8. Children's Privacy

This application is not directed to children under the age of 13. No personal information is knowingly collected from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in operations or legal requirements. The updated version will be posted at the same URL with a revised effective date.
